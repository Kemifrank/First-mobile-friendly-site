Enugu Golf Course

Enugu Golf Club plays host to both local and international tournaments in collaboration with Golf Associations around the country. An ideal place for relaxation during and after the tournament, the club has a beautiful 18 hole golf course with an all year round season which has been become a solid attraction for both locals and tourists alike. Enugu Golf Club also features a clubhouse and a well stocked bar.

Enugu Foods

Enjoying the local food and cuisine of your host is a must do for every visitor or intending visitor, every country, state or city is known for one particular food or the other. The Igbo people of Nigeria are known for numerous traditional food that are exclusively theirs and most of these food has its origin and are best known with the people of Enugu. Enugu no doubt is the home of cuisine in Nigeria and Igbo land in particular. Okpa Okpa, also referred to as king’s meal or lion’s food is a popular Igbo dish of Eastern Nigeria which originated from Enugu. Okpa is made from bambara flour gotten from bambara nut.
